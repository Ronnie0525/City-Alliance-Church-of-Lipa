Ministry · Evangelism

Sharing the Hope of Jesus With Our City

Lipa is our mission field. We long to see the gospel travel from our pews into every barangay, every workplace, every coffee shop conversation — until the name of Jesus is sweet to everyone in this city.

How We Reach Out

Four Front Lines

Community Outreach

Feeding programs, livelihood training, and tutorials in underserved barangays — gospel and good deeds, never one without the other.

Personal Witness

Training our members to share their own faith with confidence, kindness, and clarity — at work, at home, online.

Prayer Walks

Walking the streets of Lipa, praying over schools, hospitals, and neighborhoods. Asking God to do what only He can.

Mission Partnerships

Where We Send and Support

Rural Batangas

Quarterly mission teams plant simple house churches and disciple new believers in nearby provinces.

Prison Ministry

Monthly visits to a regional facility — Bible studies, worship, and follow-up with men preparing for release.

Overseas Missionaries

We pray for and financially support five families serving as missionaries across Southeast Asia.

Campus Ministry

Partnership with student fellowships at two Lipa-area universities — Bible studies, retreats, mentoring.

Marketplace Ministry

Equipping believers to share Christ in offices, sari-sari stores, jeepney routes, and everywhere else.

Disaster Response

Mobilizing relief and gospel presence whenever typhoons, floods, or other crises strike our region.

Training

Equipping Everyday Witnesses

Evangelism isn't a gift only the bold and loud receive — it is the call of every Christian. Our six-week training walks you through the gospel, common questions, and how to share your own story. By the end, you'll have practiced sharing Jesus with three real people. Most who finish say it's one of the best things they've ever done.
