Ministry · Discipleship

Following Christ — One Step at a Time

Discipleship is not a class to complete; it's a life to live. Wherever you are on the road — exploring, struggling, growing, leading — we want to walk that road with you. Real change happens slowly, in the company of brothers and sisters.

The Pathway

Three Steps on the Road

1. Explore

For seekers and new believers. We open the basics of the faith — who Jesus is, what the gospel means, and how to read the Bible — in honest, no-judgment conversations.

2. Grow

For believers ready to go deeper. One-on-one or small group mentoring through prayer, Scripture, identity in Christ, and life rhythms that form a disciple.

3. Multiply

For those ready to disciple others. We equip you to mentor the next person — because the gospel was always meant to be passed on.

How It Works

The Mentor Relationship

We pair you with someone a few steps further along on the journey — a believer who has walked through some of what you are walking through. You meet weekly or bi-weekly, work through a simple guide together, and pray for each other. It is not therapy and it is not a curriculum; it is friendship with intent.

What We Study

Core Topics

The Gospel

The story Christianity tells about God, the world, ourselves, and Jesus — and why it changes everything.

Identity in Christ

Who you actually are once you are in Jesus — and how that truth reshapes how you live each day.

Prayer & Scripture

Practical, sustainable rhythms for reading the Bible and praying — not legalism, but love.

Community

Why we cannot follow Jesus alone — and how to give and receive grace within the church.

Mission

Living as sent ones in your home, workplace, and neighborhood — without becoming weird.

Suffering & Hope

How the gospel speaks to grief, doubt, and the seasons when God feels far. Real answers, no clichés.
