First Time With Us?

Here's What Sunday Looks Like

Warm Welcome

Our hospitality team meets you at the door with a smile, a hot cup of coffee, and answers to any question you might have. No pressure, no awkward introductions.

Honest Worship

About ninety minutes of singing, prayer, Scripture, and a grounded message from the Bible. We sing both hymns and modern songs — and you can simply listen if you'd rather.

Family-Friendly

Children are welcome in the service, and we offer a fun, safe kids' program during the 10:30 AM service. Parents of toddlers can use our quiet room with live audio.

A Word From Our Pastor

Meet Pastor Rex

Rex Dela Rosa

Church Pastor

Dear friend, wherever you are in life right now — whether you have walked with Jesus for many years or you have never stepped into a church — you are welcome here. My deepest joy is to tell you that Jesus Christ is alive, He loves you, and salvation is freely offered to anyone who calls on His name.

If you have not yet given your life to Christ, there is no better day than today. He saves, He sanctifies, He heals, and He is coming again. And if you already know Him, the journey only grows sweeter as we walk it together — worshiping, learning, serving, and belonging to one another in the family of God.

We would be honored to walk with you. Come and join us this Sunday — there is a seat saved for you, and the joy of the gospel waiting to be shared.

"If you declare with your mouth, 'Jesus is Lord,' and believe in your heart that God raised him from the dead, you will be saved." — Romans 10:9

— Pastor Rex Dela Rosa
